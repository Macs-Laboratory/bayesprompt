*Note: Baselines marked as "External Template" (e.g., VP-SFDA, MAUP, MedSAM-U, CDUN) represent external benchmark checkpoints or outputs reported in the paper. They are tracked in the codebase to prevent fabricating results that require missing external checkpoints.*

### Table Structure & Purposes
- **Main Dice Table:** A broad cross-modality comparison of segmentation performance across $k$-shot settings ($k \in \{1, 3, 5, 10\}$) as reported in the camera-ready paper.
- **Component Ablation Table:** Evaluates the contributions of BMPA, PPM, and uncertainty weighting, primarily on the internal RCT setting reported in the camera-ready paper.
- **Calibration/Stability Table:** Reports Dice/ECE reliability comparisons against SAM2 Fine-tune, MedSAM-U, Prompt-only, and BayesPrompt on the public and internal settings included in the camera-ready paper.
- **Parameter-Efficiency Table:** Optional repository-side profiling of trainable vs. frozen parameter budgets, if generated by the efficiency runner.
